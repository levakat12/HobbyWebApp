Hobby Tracker

Track every hobby in one focused place. Scroll down to see how Hobby Tracker helps you organize goals, log sessions, and stay consistent over time.

What It Proposes

Hobby Tracker is designed as a personal progress console for hobbyists. Instead of scattered notes and forgotten plans, you get one timeline to track effort, results, and momentum with clear structure.

How It Works

  • Create each hobby with your own goals and focus area.
  • Log sessions with time spent, notes, and milestones reached.
  • Monitor streaks and consistency through visual progress snapshots.
  • Review history to understand what routines actually work for you.

Built For Consistency

The goal is simple: help you keep showing up. Start with one hobby, build the habit, and let your progress history guide your next step.
